Traditional Birria Taco Recipe

Ingredients

For the Meat:

  • 3 lbs beef chuck roast
  • 2 lbs goat meat (optional)
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp black pepper

For the Adobo:

  • 4 guajillo chiles
  • 2 ancho chiles
  • 3 chipotle chiles
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • Mexican oregano

Instructions

  1. Toast the dried chiles until fragrant
  2. Soak chiles in hot water for 30 minutes
  3. Blend chiles with spices to make adobo
  4. Marinate meat for 4-24 hours
  5. Cook in Dutch oven for 3-4 hours
  6. Shred meat and prepare consommé

Pro Tips

  • Use high-quality meat
  • Don't rush the marinade
  • Strain the consommé twice
  • Keep tacos warm while serving

Serving

Serve with:

  • Corn tortillas
  • Diced onions
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges
  • Hot consommé
