Essential Tips

  • Toast your chiles for deeper, more complex flavor profiles
  • Use a mixture of beef and goat for traditional Jalisco-style taste
  • Marinate meat for at least 4 hours in adobo sauce
  • Dip tortillas in consommé before grilling for that signature red color
  • Serve with lime, onions, and cilantro for authentic presentation
